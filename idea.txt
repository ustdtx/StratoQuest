Strato Quest: An adventure across the sky


Strato Quest: An adventure across the sky is a 3d video game where you fight across the skies defending earth from an alien invasion. Across levels you come by various powerups and harder challenges with various enemies. 

Features:
1) 3d stages with various obstacles and a jet as the player model and various enemy combinations.
2) 3d enemies with variations in behaviour and attack pattern
3) Health count and recharge
4) Shield and shield pickup
5) Booster that gain temporary invincibility
6) Temporary attack speed increase
7) Special Attack: Missiles & Laser
8) Coin Pickup for extra score
9) FInal boss fight giving a thrilling ending
